About Us

Experienced. Certified. Dedicated to Your Success.

At Colonial Driving School, we provide top-tier driving instruction to the entire Richmond metropolitan area. As a fully certified, full-service driving school, we offer courses for new drivers, experienced drivers looking to improve, and those needing ticket dismissal or defensive driving training.
